What is Meta-print


Metaprint is a pioneering project that transforms 3D data into NFTs and converts these NFTs into physical artworks through 3D printing. By merging digital and physical art, Metaprint offers innovative forms of artistic expression and interaction.


This unique platform enhances the value and significance of digital assets, providing artists and collectors with a seamless bridge between the virtual and real worlds. The theme is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.

Buy NFT

On Metaprint, buying NFTs is easy and secure. Browse unique digital artworks, make your purchase, and optionally transform your NFT into a physical piece with our high-quality 3D printing service. Enjoy the best of both digital and physical worlds.

Sell NFT

On Metaprint,Selling your NFTs is simple and rewarding. Upload your digital artwork, mint it as a unique NFT, and list it on our marketplace. Reach a global audience of collectors and enthusiasts, and enhance your art’s value by offering the option to transform it into a physical piece with our 3D printing service.
